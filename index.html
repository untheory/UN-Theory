ABOUT UN THEORY

UN Theory is a complete theoretical framework describing the Unified Information Substrate: the δI‑microstructure, emergent geometry, quantum behaviour, cosmological dynamics, sparse‑regime planetary structure, ultra‑global correlation physics, and the terminal‑state architecture of reality. The theory provides a single, continuous structure linking quantum behaviour, cosmological evolution, planetary‑scale sparse‑regime dynamics, ultra‑global fixed‑point geometry, and the terminal‑state return to I(x, x′).

This site serves as the primary public landing page for UN Theory. It provides a stable, authoritative index of all published materials, including Zenodo DOIs, sector papers, formal appendices, and cross‑volume documentation. Every document is cross‑linked through ORCID, Zenodo, and GitHub to ensure persistent academic attribution, long‑term discoverability, and correct identity resolution.
